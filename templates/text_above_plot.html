VisualLie is a web app to visualize the root system of the Feingold-Frenkel algebra. It comes in the form of a single VisualLie.html file that can be opened with any modern web browser.

The Feingold-Frenkel algebra \(\mathcal{F}\) (or \(AE_3\) or \(A_1^{++}\)). It is a rank 3 hyperbolic Kac-Moody algebra associated with the indefinite Cartan matrix

\[ A = (a_{ij}) = \begin{pmatrix}2 & -1 & 0 \\ -1 & 2 & -2 \\ 0 & -2 & 2 \end{pmatrix} \, . \]

Its study was pioneered in [1]. A slightly more comprehensive introduction to the Feingold-Frenkel algebra \(\mathcal{F}\) is given below the figure (to be added later).

The figure shows the root system of \(\mathcal{F}\) for one level at a time. The figure is fully interactive. Hover over a root to see its root vector, norm, and multiplicity. Click on a root to see its Weyl orbit. Use the bokeh plot tools on the right to pan, zoom, save, or reset the plot. Use the selectors, checkboxes, and buttons on the left to change the level of the visualized roots and turn on additional plot features.
